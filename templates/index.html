ENGINEERING
LABORATORY

Engineering laboratory is a specialized space equipped with tools, instruments, and technology for conducting experiments, research, and practical applications in various engineering fields. It allows students and professionals to apply theoretical knowledge, test designs, and develop innovative solutions to real-world problems.

GUIDELINES

Laboratory Guidelines and Policies

Laboratory Guidelines

Laboratory guidelines ensure a structured and efficient working environment by requiring proper conduct, the use of personal protective equipment (PPE), and adherence to equipment handling protocols. They emphasize cleanliness, organization, and strict compliance with standard operating procedures to maintain order and productivity.

Safety and Policies

Safety policies focus on preventing accidents by enforcing hazard identification, emergency preparedness, and strict chemical and biological safety protocols. They require immediate incident reporting, proper waste disposal, and continuous adherence to safety measures to protect individuals and the environment.

MISSION AND OBJECTIVES

Mission

The laboratory aims to provide a well-equipped, safe, and accessible environment for students and faculty to enhance learning, innovation, and research. Through modern facilities and an efficient equipment management system, we strive to support hands-on experimentation, technical skills development, and collaboration in various fields of study.

Objectives

  • Provide Easy Access – Enable students to reserve and borrow equipment online.
  • Ensure Safety – Enforce laboratory rules and safety protocols.
  • Manage Resources Efficiently – Maintain an organized inventory system.
  • Support Learning & Research – Facilitate hands-on experiments and innovation.
  • Improve Communication – Offer updates, assistance, and lab support.
